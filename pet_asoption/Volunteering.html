Becoming a Volunteer

To volunteer with Tabby Cats Rescue, you must fulfil the following conditions:

  • Over 15 years of age.
  • If you are under 18 years of age we will require permission to volunteer with us from a parent/guardian.

If you are interested in a college / school work placement with us, please email us on tabbycatsrescue@gmail.com.

We have a few different roles available, including:

  • Events Organiser
    As a voluntary events organiser you’ll play a key role in organising and running our awareness and fundraising events. Working in conjunction with our Shelter management team, you’ll be involved in the planning of our events as well as ensuring that everything runs as smoothly as possible. This voluntary role will suit a person who has helped organise events before, or who is studying for an events management career. For more details, please email us.

    Events Volunteer
    As an events volunteer you’ll assist an events organiser at our exciting events across Yorkshire throughout the year. 2024 marks the 20th anniversary of the Tabby Cats Rescue, and we’ll be celebrating in style. Our events will provide a great opportunity for us to spread the message about our vital work as well as contributing to our fundraising efforts. This role is perfect for enthusiastic volunteers who love cats, love events and love meeting people. Experience is great, but enthusiasm is even more important!
